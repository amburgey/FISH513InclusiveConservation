Speakers for FISH 513

We are extremely privileged to have a fantastic lineup of speakers for this seminar. To learn more about each specific speaker and their work, see their individual biographies below.

Public Seminar

Each speaker will talk about their work in a publicly-viewable and recorded seminar for the first 30 minutes of the class. Attendees can submit questions for the speaker via Zoom. Depending on time, the speaker will answer several questions during this public portion.

Class Discussion

After the talk, non-enrolled attendees will be asked to leave. Discussion with speakers will be structured around student-generated questions from weekly readings and from content presented in the public talk.
